American Eagle Foundation

This Tennessee charity is protecting the majestic Bald Eagle, America’s National Symbol, through environmental recovery and education programs

Clearwater Marine Aquarium

Based in The Sunshine State of Florida, the Clearwater Marine Aquarium works to preserve marine life and environment through education, research, rescue, rehabilitation and release.

Tri-CED Community Recycling | Our Youth

We work with community-based organizations, churches, youth services agencies, local police, the juvenile justice system and families to identify prospective youth who might benefit from the opportunity.









The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change

Reports The IPCC prepares comprehensive Assessment Reports about the state of scientific, technical and socio-economic knowledge on climate change, its impacts and future risks, and options for reducing the rate at which climate change is taking place.

Green Climate Fund

GCF is a unique global platform to respond to climate change by investing in low-emission and climate-resilient development. GCF was established by 194 governments to limit or reduce greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions in developing countries, and to help vulnerable societies adapt to the unavoidable impacts of climate change.

Climate Investment Funds

is one of the world’s leading climate finance mechanisms. Founded in 2008, it represents one of the first global efforts to invest in a dedicated climate finance.














What will be the state of planet Earth in 50 years?
  • As far as global warming goes, the Dead Sea will be almost completely dried out, more than half of Amazon rain forest will have been deforested, wildfires will spread, and the polar ice caps will be only 50-60 percent the size they are now.
  • Rising temperatures mean that heat stress will place a significant strain on human labour productivity.
  • Super hurricanes will become a regular occurrence, which will eventually lead to the permanent abandonment of cities on the Gulf Coast.
Things You Can Do to Help Save the Earth
  • Conserve Water

    • The little things can make a big difference. Every time you turn off the water while you're brushing your teeth, you're doing something good

  • Be Car-conscious

    • If you can, stay off the road two days a week or more. You'll reduce greenhouse gas emissions by an average of 1,590 pounds (721 kilograms) per year [source: ]. It's easier than you think

  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

    • You can help reduce pollution just by putting that soda can in the bin. It really does make a difference. Paper, too.
